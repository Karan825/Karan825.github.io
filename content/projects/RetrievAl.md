Architected an end-to-end Intelligent Candidate Ranking Pipeline using a 4-bit quantized Qwen-2.5 (1.5B) via Llama.cpp and BGE-Small embeddings, automating structured intent extraction and semantic retrieval from raw Job Descriptions. Engineered a dual-vector semantic scoring pipeline with a custom skills-grounding engine and honeypot filters to detect synthetic profiles. Optimized CPU inference to achieve a 1,700+ candidates/second processing throughput.
